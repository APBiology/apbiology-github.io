AP Biology: Evolution

Introduction

Evolution, often simply recognized as “change”, is an accepted truth. We can see from history that species have changed, including our very ancestors that speciated from another species. Despite this common understanding, there is much debate of whether evolution, in its more detailed biological definition “a change in the genetic composition of a population from generation to generation”, is true. In other words, the answer to whether Darwin’s theory of evolution is true or not known. At this point in history, however, contemporary scientists (especially biologists) and those in the past had based their research and development by applying the Darwinian theory that was published in 1859. Material evidence suddenly seemed more transparent as technology developed and scientists could study lifeforms in more detail and clearer views. Some scientists, however, still concluded it false, and we do not know it for a sure thing.

AP Biology, and as much as most biologists are concerned believe in Darwinian evolution as a truth. The concept of natural selection was groundbreaking and still is a leading factor in biological studies today. Through this e-book, we will be discussing the many factors that explain and prove why Darwinian evolution is a concept proved through experiment and observation. Before we dive into the deeper details, let us take a quick review of what things were like before Darwinian evolution.

 

Through this, I hope you can learn the evolution of the concept of Biological evolution through the perspective of multiple scientists and particularly of Darwin.
