Our review range will be purely based off of the AP Biology Course and Exam Description (Effective Autumn 2015).

 

Since the exam essentially revolves around the concepts and examples given, it is crucial for students to understand and attempt to break down the AP Biology exam into 4 Big Ideas and thousands of essential knowledges.

 

For the purpose of this website, we will only cover Big Idea 1: "The process of evolution drives the diversity and unity of life".
